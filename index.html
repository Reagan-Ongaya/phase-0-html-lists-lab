• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tdsp of butter

Steps of making grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on the bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in fring pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of the bread
  4. Cover the second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
